// Token launchpad

Launch a token. Sell it fairly. Build a market.

punk.fun is a token launchpad. You create a token, run a falling-price auction to sell it, and the proceeds automatically seed a trading pool so the token can be traded freely afterwards. This site explains all of that in plain English — no smart-contract knowledge required.

What punk.fun does

Most token launches have the same two problems: setting a fair starting price is hard, and there's often no real market to trade in once the launch ends. punk.fun is built to solve both in a single flow.

Every launch moves through three stages:

  1. CreateA token is created with a fixed supply. The full supply goes to the person launching it.
  2. AuctionHalf of the supply is sold through a Dutch auction — the price starts high and falls over time until buyers step in. Buyers commit funds during the auction and collect their tokens once it ends.
  3. SettleWhen the sale finishes, the money raised and the other half of the supply are automatically placed into a trading pool. The token is now freely tradeable.
The short version

You don't have to guess a price, you don't have to manually create a market afterwards, and buyers never overpay — the auction finds the price for you.

Two launch styles

punk.fun supports two kinds of launch. They share the same auction engine but differ in where the token comes from and what happens to liquidity afterwards.

StyleBest forToken source
FUN Quick community / memecoin launches A token created through punk.fun's own token creator
PUNK Soon Projects that already have their own token Any existing token you bring yourself

A note on risk

Buying newly launched tokens is risky. Prices can move sharply and there is no guarantee a token holds value. This documentation explains how the mechanics work — it is not financial advice. Only ever commit funds you are prepared to lose.
